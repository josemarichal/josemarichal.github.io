Course Goals

This course aims to build a "global affairs toolkit" to help you understand the complex and uncertain international landscape. We will cover:

  • Fundamental lenses of global politics
  • Political and economic systems
  • Globalization vs. nationalism
  • The impact of AI and technology on the global order
