Course Goals

This course investigates how data collection (social media) and analysis (AI) are fundamentally reshaping liberal democracy. We will explore:

  • The shift from "Rights" to "Optimization"
  • The "Machine Liberalism" social contract
  • A "Living Syllabus" with guest scholars from CITAP
  • Participatory AI Lab: Using AI tools to solve local problems
