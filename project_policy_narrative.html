Policy Narrative Analyst

Decomposing Political Speech with AI

Project Overview

The **Policy Narrative Analyst** is an AI-powered tool designed to systematically deconstruct political speeches, reports, and policy documents. It leverages advanced Large Language Models (LLMs) to automate the "Plot in Policy Narratives" framework.

By extracting structured data on narrative elements, this tool helps researchers and students move beyond surface-level reading to uncover the deeper storytelling mechanics used to frame public policy issues.

Launch the Narrative Analyst Gem

The Framework: Plot in Policy Narratives

Based on established narrative policy frameworks, the tool analyzes text across three critical dimensions:

  • 1. Structure (Sequence): How is time organized? Where does the story begin and end? What is the overarching plot archetype (e.g., Decline-Crisis-Rebirth)?
  • 2. Valence (Polarity): How are policy problems defined? What emotional tones (fear, hope, anger) are deployed? How is thick description used to evoke sentiment?
  • 3. Causality (Explanation): Who are the heroes, villains, and victims? What causal mechanisms are asserted to link problems to solutions?

How It Works

  1. Launch the Gem: Click the button above to open the Gemini Gem interface (requires a Gemini Advanced subscription).
  2. Paste Your Text: Input any political speech, policy brief, or article.
  3. Get Instant Analysis: The AI will generate a structured report breaking down the narrative components (Structure, Valence, Causality) with citations from the text.
