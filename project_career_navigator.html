Career Path Navigator

Acting Like a Local in Your Future Field

Project Overview

Starting a career is disorienting in exactly the same way moving to a new city is. You don't know the layout, you don't know anyone, and you're trying to figure out where the good spots are before anyone figures out you're new.

The **Career Path Navigator** is a Gemini Gem designed to help students transition from "university tourists" to "industry locals" using a structured 7-step networking and presence framework.

Launch the Career Navigator Gem

The "Acting Like a Local" Framework

The tool guides you through seven interactive modules to build genuine credibility and community:

  • 1. Buy Local (Presence): Audit your LinkedIn and signals to show you "live here now."
  • 2. Get a Map (Landscape): Sketch out the landscape of major players and boutiques using Python analysis.
  • 3. Buy Lunch (Outreach): Draft informational interview requests that professionals actually want to answer.
  • 4. Play Hard (Community): Engineering repeated contact in low-stakes environments like hackathons or clubs.
  • 5. Jesus and Wine (Subcultures): Finding your specific tribe and enthusiasts within the field.
  • 6. Coffee, Tea, Me (Ambient Exposure): Hanging out where your field hangs out online.
  • 7. Get Involved (Ownership): Taking ownership of unglamorous but vital tasks for organizations in your field.

How It Works

  1. Launch the Gem: Click the button above and tell the AI what industry you are targeting.
  2. Pick a Module: Choose one of the 7 sections of the "Acting Like a Local" framework.
  3. Execute Homework: Every session ends with a small, actionable task to build your local presence.
